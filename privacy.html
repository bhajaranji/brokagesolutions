Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2025

Welcome to Stackly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we protect it, and your rights.

1. Scope & Acceptance

This policy applies to all Stackly platforms and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information
  • Property listing & documents
  • Transaction & payment records
  • Verification & screening data
  • Usage & analytics data

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All data uses TLS secure transfer.
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive documents stored in encrypted systems.
  • Access Control: Only authorized staff with MFA access.
  • Monitoring: Automated threat detection & activity logs.
  • Backups: Encrypted daily backups with disaster recovery.
